📜 RULES OF GAME

🎲 Gameplay Rules

1. Roll the dice and move on the physical board.

2. Teacher selects the tile type based on where the player lands.

3. Players answer the mathematics challenge.

4. Correct answer gives points and rewards.

5. Crystal Space gives crystal currency.

6. Math Crystals are unlocked through mathematics performance.
